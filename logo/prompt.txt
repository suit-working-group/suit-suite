Voici trois prompts prêts à utiliser. Je les mets en anglais, car les générateurs visuels comprennent souvent mieux les consignes de logo en anglais. Je recommande de demander un symbole sans texte d’abord, puis d’ajouter “SUIT” proprement ensuite avec une vraie typographie.

Proposition 1 — Très minimaliste

Create a minimalist black and white logo mark for “SUIT — Sustainable University IT Infrastructure & Technology”.

The logo should be a simple abstract symbol arranged as an inverted pyramid with three centered horizontal levels.

Top level: three hollow circles. The first two circles are connected by a thin straight line. The third circle is separate and not connected to the first two, but aligned on the same row.

Middle level: two small filled triangles connected by a thin straight line. The triangles should be centered below the top row and slightly closer together.

Bottom level: one filled square centered inside a larger hollow square. There must be generous empty space between the inner filled square and the outer square border, expressing isolation and containment.

Use only black shapes on a white background. Keep the design flat, geometric, balanced, institutional, and highly legible at small sizes. No gradients, no shadows, no 3D effects, no icons, no text, no leaf, no globe, no shield, no padlock.

Proposition 2 — Plus institutionnelle

Design a sober institutional logo mark for “SUIT — Sustainable University IT Infrastructure & Technology”.

The mark must represent graduated levels of digital freedom and constraint in a university IT governance framework. Use a centered inverted pyramid composition with three levels.

The first level represents open academic action: three hollow circles in a horizontal row, with only the first and second circles connected by a thin line. The third circle remains intentionally unconnected to show independent action.

The second level represents structured but still open action: two filled equilateral triangles connected by a thin line, centered below the first level.

The third level represents a highly secured isolated context: a solid filled square placed inside a larger empty square outline, with clear empty space between the inner square and the outer border.

Use a strict monochrome style: black on white. The result should feel like a serious reference-document logo, suitable for university governance documents, LaTeX PDFs, GitHub repositories, and executive presentations. Keep the geometry precise, calm, and uncluttered. Do not include decorative technology icons, environmental symbols, cybersecurity clichés, shields, locks, clouds, leaves, or globes.

Proposition 3 — Géométrique / moderne, avec versions light et dark

Create a modern geometric monochrome logo mark for “SUIT — Sustainable University IT Infrastructure & Technology”.

The symbol should be composed of three centered rows forming an inverted pyramid.

Row 1: three hollow circular nodes. Connect only the first two nodes with a thin line. Leave the third node unconnected but visually aligned with the others.

Row 2: two filled triangular nodes, connected by a thin horizontal line. This row should be centered under the first row and more compact.

Row 3: one containment symbol: a solid square centered inside a larger outlined square, with generous spacing between the solid inner square and the outer square outline.

The visual meaning is: broad freedom of action, then structured action, then isolated high-security containment. Make the logo extremely simple, scalable, and readable as a small icon.

Provide two versions: a light version with black shapes on a white background, and a dark version with white shapes on a black background. Use flat vector style, clean geometry, consistent stroke weight, no text, no gradients, no shadows, no 3D, no decorative elements, no padlock, no shield, no globe, no leaf.
